Our Purpose

PMSA is a non‑profit national organization that is led by a volunteer executive board with regional chapter representatives. We welcome students from all medical schools and from all backgrounds. We aim to:

  • Build community and belonging across institutions.
  • Advance mentorship pipelines from pre‑med to residency.
  • Support leadership development and advocacy for health equity.
  • Celebrate heritage and cultural identity in medicine.

Core Programs

Mentorship

Vertical mentorship across M1 – M4.

Research Network

Project feedback and multi‑site collaborations.

Professional Development

CV building workshops and specialty exploration.

Service & Outreach

Community health fairs and culturally competent care initiatives.
